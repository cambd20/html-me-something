Best Things About KC

From what I can understand, a lot of people outside of the midwest don't know much about KC or simply just have a misunderstanding about the city. For example, many don't even realize that most of the city is located in Missouri as opposed to Kansas. I have composed a list of 5 great things that KC has to offer!

Museums

Explore places like the Nelson Atkins Museum, The National World War 1 Museum, The College Basketball Experience, to see beautiful displays and learn an in-depth history of each category respectively

Beer

Go take a tour at the Boulevard Brewing Company and learn how some of the best beer in the US originated. There is plenty of tasting involved as well with beer such as Boulevard Wheat, Pale Ale, Space Camper, Berry Jam; there is surely a little something for everyone!

Power and Light District

An area filled with tasty restaurants, great bars, and where live concerts are held, this place is one of the biggest attractions for people of all ages and types!

BBQ

There are literally hundreds of barbecue places scattered across the metro, all with their own unique style. Places like Q39, KC Joe's, Jack Stack, and Gates are sure to send you home feeling satisfied. I, personally, am a Jack Stack advocate. However, that may be a bit biased since that is my current place of employment.
